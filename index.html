Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Wear protective gear, follow traffic rules, maintain your bike, stay alert, and continuously improve your riding skills. Safety and awareness are paramount for a fulfilling journey.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

Inspect for signs of wear or damage: cracks, dents, faded colors, loose padding, or a frayed strap. Helmets typically last 5-7 years, replace sooner if damaged or after a crash.

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride?

Before riding a bike in storage, check the tires for pressure, inspect brakes, test lights, and ensure fluid levels. Consider a thorough inspection by a professional mechanic for safety.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

When riding a bike, follow traffic rules, wear protective gear, signal turns, check mirrors, maintain speed limits, and stay aware of your surroundings for a safe ride.
